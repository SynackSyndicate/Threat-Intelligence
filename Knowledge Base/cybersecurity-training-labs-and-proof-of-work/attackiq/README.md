# ATTACKIQ

AttackIQ is one of the leaders of Purple Teaming. Now for those of you who don't know, Purple Team is the blend of Blue and Red Teams (_duh_). In all seriousness, Purple Team does an excellent job explaining and guiding those who are new to the Cyber field in to a more broader landscape. They cover how to map MITRE, learn MITRE ATT\&CK and CAR repositories, etc. Basically helps you understand a cyber attack, acknowledge it, and the types of mitigations or how-tos to end the process and mitigate the damage.
