# Intro to Digital Forensics

SBTs Intro to Digital Forensics helped me understand how to dig further into malicious malware. Threat hunter and Digital forensics I believe have the same fundamental goals as one another. It's to find, kill, mitigate, and repair malware and src\_ips on the network. Digital forensics help cyber practitioners read between the lines, and find out the details left behind through digital footprints.&#x20;
