# RangeForce

RangeForce is a cyber range geared towards up and coming cyber professionals looking to get their feet wet or leveling up their career. They offer many different Battle Paths from Cybersecurity Essentials, and to their new Threat Hunting courses. These modules help simulate real world events where SOCs are meant to set up alerts, to investigating problems and mitigating damages. \
