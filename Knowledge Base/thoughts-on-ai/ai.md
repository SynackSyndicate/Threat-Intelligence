### Belief

I believe the market will 'correct' itself but not in a way many entry people would like to hope. The naysayers saying AI won't take our Jobs, **it most certainly will, can, and restrict**.&#x20;

For instance, I was working an Incident Response and we were able to obtain a snapshot of the Database. The database was SQL and pulled up in a text editor file. We put that hot mess of a SQL DB into Claude and cross analyzed it with our contextual evidence - _**boom**_ a fully fledge IR report. We of course read it to confirm and reconfirm our analysis. And it was spot on. One IR that would've taken about a week to parse, analyze, cross reference — take into account our additional workload — was done in 10 minutes. We were all baffled, shocked, and stunned at the speed and rate this took. **Personally,** i miss actually parsing, digging, cross referencing, and creating a meaningful report. It becomes actually part of your soul; work you hold dear to your heart. Saying "_yeah i did that sh-"_ and having actual postive feedback on your work. Now? Everything will be prefaced "_so i grabbed these artifacts, put them in AI, re read the report, ready to ship boss"_ yuck.

### Identity

AI is another Identity hands down. It needs to be regulated as a seperate entity in your Roles / idP or whatever its used for in your environment. Its a cost that needs to be firmly regulated or else it gets out of hand like the article above where tokens are getting burned like an arcade. And depending on that role, it should have either READ\_ONLY privileges and/or Departmental Groups that have the WRITE Function with it as well.
