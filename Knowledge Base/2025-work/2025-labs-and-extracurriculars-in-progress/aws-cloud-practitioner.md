# AWS Cloud Practitioner

Completed my AWS Cloud Practitioner "Foundational" Exam. Overall this exam covers a plethora of services within AWS. I'd like to say that I had no formal experience regarding AWS, and I had just started tinkering with it through my job about 3 months ago at the time of this writing. I did have a Cloud Certification from INE back in 2022(?) but that was it. \
\
This exam was an inch deep and mile wide. There's tons of services and jargon that was associated with it. I honestly thought that I was screwed with all of the types of services and the definitions for what they do. The best thing I can say is to try to go through the course material from AWS once. Then look over this youtube playlist \
\
[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m95IMRN1sqM\&t=1738s](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m95IMRN1sqM\&t=1738s)&#x20;

\
Overall the test was quite easy, its a lot of deducing the wrong answers and picking the best case scenario between the two. \
\
Know your CAF Frameworks, Security _IN_ the cloud and Security _OF_ the Cloud, and know a few scenario questions. \
